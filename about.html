The Origin

Why We
Started

DNA Sportswear was not built from a business plan. It was built it from the touchlines, the changing rooms and heaps of coffee. Growing up in sport, we were tired of kit that didn’t reflect the teams wearing it. Slow lead times. Generic designs. Service that didn’t deliver.

We knew sport deserved better, So, we created a new kind of teamwear brand. One that was fast, focused and built around the people who wear the kit. What started with a few machines, and a lot of energy has grown into a trusted name in custom sportswear. We now supply schools, clubs and corporates with kit made in Cape Town and engineered to push beyond limits.

And we’ve still got the same pride and obsession ever since our first sketch.

Our Process

How We
Work

Refined Process. Precise Delivery. This Is What Sets Us Apart.
We don’t believe in long timelines or unclear communication. From day one, our process is straightforward, and our team supports you every step of the way. Whether you’re placing your first order or outfitting your entire team or firm, we make it effortless. We manage your enquiry, develop your concepts, and oversee production from start to finish.

01
Enquire & Brief

Fill in our enquiry form with your sport, team size and rough timeline. Your dedicated account manager gets back to you within.

02
Design & Approve

Our design team creates artwork based on your brief. You review, request changes if needed, and sign off. Usually sorted in 2–3 rounds.

03
Production

Once approved, your kit goes into production. We manufacture locally, so we can keep quality tight and timelines short.

04
Delivery

Kit is checked, packed and dispatched. You get tracking details and your order arrives within the agreed window - no surprises.
